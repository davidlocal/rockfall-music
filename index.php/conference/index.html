    • Jon McCarthy

    Jon McCarthy serves on the team at Rockfall Music and is at the helm at hosting Rockfall events and conferences. He is a former singer and released his first single, "My Bonnie". The single went widespread around Bootupulis and propelled him to prominence. The single led to his first extended play released in 2018 entitled, "Frontiers". Prior to its release, another single came forth from it entitled, "Good Feeling", which also became a popular hit. Jon resides in Bootupulis, CA and loves serving in other areas besides hosting at Rockfall Music.
